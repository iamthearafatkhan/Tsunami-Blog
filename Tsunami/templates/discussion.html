Community Resilience

Building community resilience involves education, planning, and training. Engaging local populations in tsunami preparedness initiatives fosters a culture of safety and awareness.

Tsunami Warning Systems

Modern technology enables rapid detection of tsunamis through seismic activity monitoring. Tsunami warning systems provide critical alerts to coastal communities, potentially saving lives and minimizing damage.

Scientific Research on Tsunamis

Scientists study tsunamis to understand their patterns and improve early warning systems. Research focuses on tsunami generation, wave propagation, and the impact on coastal ecosystems.
